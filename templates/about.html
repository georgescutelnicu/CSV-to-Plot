About CSV to Chart

Welcome to CSV to Chart, a web application designed to streamline the process of visualizing data from CSV files. Whether you're a data enthusiast, analyst, or someone working with CSV files, our tool provides a user-friendly interface to transform your raw data into insightful charts and graphs.

  • Effortlessly upload CSV files.
  • Analyze datasets visually using various chart types.
  • Export and share visualized data seamlessly.

Our mission is to empower users to make data-driven decisions by offering a straightforward and effective visualization tool.

The following type of visualizations are available: Bar Chart, Pie Chart, Scatter Plot, Line Chart, Histogram.

If the dataset does not contain the required columns for a specific chart, you won't be able to generate it.
